Catering

Yes, we cater! Luna Maes catering kitchen can handle any size event up to 500 people. From baby showers to wedding reception desserts, to corporate events ... we can supply you with reliable, timely high quality desserts and pastries.

Book a time for a call and we can discuss your needs and plan a dessert menu. Make it a perfect Belgian inspired experience, or go more mainstream. We can supply any pastries, pies, tarts or cakes you may want. We bake it all inhouse using the finest organic ingredients.

Stop by and meet us and our friendly staff (see our hours and location here). We can also supply serving staff for your event. From casual to gala, we've got you covered.
