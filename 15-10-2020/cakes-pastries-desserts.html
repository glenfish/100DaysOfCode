DID YOU KNOW?

Belgium is known for its diversity of coffee beans, being a hub of coffee trade. Antwerp has the world's largest green (unroasted) coffee bean storage. 250,000 tonnes of coffee beans. That's 27 billion cups!

The Belgian Chocolate industry is almost 400 years old with around 2000 chocolatiers exporting more than 400,000 tonnes of chocolate every year.
