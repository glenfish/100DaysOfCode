A World Like No Other ...

Salut! Belgian chocolate, waffles, pastries and cookies are famous the world over. For centuries the great pastry chefs of Europe have made their pilgrimage to Brussels and beyond, to learn well kept secrets and to taste their infamous chocolate!

Luna Maes patisseries re-create that wonderful Belgian cafe experience from a time of steeped history and fine pleasures. Our patrons are transported back to the early 20th Century in the luxurious metropolitan style of the era.

Escape to provincial Belgium for a tasty Speculoos and coffee in one of our two convenient inner city locations in Melbourne and Sydney. There is a bucket list on our wall of delights that we challenge you to complete... our top 10 Belgian treats!

We also have an curated selection of teas for those who enjoy an interesting herbal brew. And of course our world famous Belgian Chocolate is always on hand, as is our Luna Maes selection of fruity jams.

Whether you're picking up a quick breakfast before work, or relaxing on the weekend, our cafe welcomes you for a unique and memorable occasion.

DID YOU KNOW?

Luna Maes was born in Veurne, Belgium in 1944. She is the founder and the inspiration behind all we do. Her vision was to bring a slice of her home town to Australia, after moving to Melbourne Victoria in 1972. She still visits the kitchen from time to time and chats with our guests.

The Luna Maes Jam product range launched in 1989 with a classic marmalade, followed by a mulberry confit. The full range is available in store at both our locations.
